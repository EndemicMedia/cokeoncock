### **Summary of Service Strategy for Europe**

1.  **Use Shirtee (Germany) or Printify (Textildruck Europa)** for your **Main Apparel** (Hoodies/Tees). They have the best access to *Stanley/Stella* stock, which is the fabric your customers actually want.
2.  **Use Printful (Latvia/Spain)** for **Headwear & Accessories**. Their embroidery quality is superior, and they have unique items like the "Embroidered Socks" and "3D Puff Hats" that the others lack.
